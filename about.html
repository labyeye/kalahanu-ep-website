project timeline

From Concept to Collection

This timeline illustrates the essential stages in the creation of a fashion collection, highlighting important milestones and deliverables.

explore

Week 1 - 2

Initial Consultation & Research

The project begins with a comprehensive client consultation to define the brand identity and target audience. Research on current market trends and competitor analysis is conducted to inform design decisions.

Week 3 - 5

Concept Development

During this phase, designers create mood boards to visually express the brand's essence. Initial sketches and design concepts are developed, followed by client feedback sessions to refine ideas and align with the client's vision.

Week 6 - 8

Design Refinement

The design concepts are finalized, and detailed digital renderings are produced. Patterns are made, and prototypes are developed for quality assurance. Client presentations are conducted to secure approval before moving forward with production.

Week 9 - 12

Production & Marketing

Once designs are approved, materials are sourced, and manufacturing is confirmed. This stage also includes the development of branding elements, such as logos and packaging. A marketing strategy is implemented to build anticipation for the upcoming collection launch.
