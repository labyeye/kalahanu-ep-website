Who We Are

About the Company

Kalahanu Bothra Manufactures Pvt Ltd is the pioneer of the Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility (RVSF) ecosystem in Bihar. As an authorized entity under the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH), we provide a scientific alternative to unorganized scrap yards.

Our facility is designed for maximum resource recovery. From draining hazardous liquids to shredding metals and sorting plastics, every step follows strict environmental protocols. We help vehicle owners transition to newer, safer, and cleaner vehicles while providing them with official certificates that unlock significant government incentives.

  • Bihar's 1st Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility
  • Scientific, automated, and eco-friendly disposal
  • Official Certificate of Deposit for tax benefits
  • Zero-waste material recovery and recycling

Everything you need to know about vehicle scrapping in Bihar

RVSF stands for Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility. It is a government-authorized facility equipped to scrap end-of-life vehicles scientifically and environment-friendly, following MoRTH guidelines.

You need the original RC (Registration Certificate), valid ID proof of the owner, and a bank NOC if the vehicle has an active loan. Our team assists with the entire documentation process.

You receive an official 'Certificate of Deposit' which entitles you to road tax waivers on new vehicle purchases, registration fee discounts, and better scrap value for your old vehicle.

Yes. We follow zero-waste material processing. All hazardous fluids (oil, coolant, battery acid) are safely drained and recycled, while metals and plastics are recovered for industrial reuse.

Once the vehicle is delivered and documents verified, the Certificate of Deposit is generated electronically on the Vahan portal, usually within 24-48 hours.
